Beginner Guidance

What is P2P Trading?
P2P (Peer-to-Peer) trading lets you buy and sell crypto directly with other users at agreed prices, with MGBX acting as an escrow to keep your funds safe.
How to Buy USDT on P2P?
1. Select "Buy" tab and choose USDT.
2. Pick an offer that suits your limit & payment method.
3. Enter amount and click Buy.
4. Pay the seller via the selected payment method.
5. Confirm payment — USDT will be released to your wallet.
How to Sell USDT on P2P?
1. Select "Sell" tab and choose USDT.
2. Pick a buy offer from a merchant.
3. Enter amount and confirm.
4. Wait for the buyer to send payment.
5. Confirm receipt — USDT will be released from escrow.
KYC & Security Settings
Complete KYC verification to unlock higher trading limits. Go to Profile → KYC Verification and submit your Aadhaar front, back, and selfie. Verification takes 24–48 hours.
Deposit & Withdrawal
Deposit crypto from any wallet to your MGBX address. Withdrawals are processed after identity verification. Minimum withdrawal limits apply per asset.

Merchant Badge Meaning

Blue V Merchant

This badge represents a high-quality merchant who has extensive experience and meets the platform's requirements for transaction volume and order completion rate. Blue V Merchants are known for their reliability and good reputation.

Crown Merchant

This is the highest-level certification for merchants on our platform. Crown Merchants have strong financial capabilities, a wealth of trading experience, and enjoy exclusive privileges.

Shield Merchants

Offer limited compensation if a seller's account is frozen due to legal reasons.
