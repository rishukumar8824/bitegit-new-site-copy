FAQs
What is Quick Buy?
Bitcovex's Quick Buy feature allows users to easily purchase cryptocurrencies using fiat currency. This feature supports multiple payment methods, including Visa, Mastercard, Google Pay, and Apple Pay, with the availability of specific payment methods depending on the selected cryptocurrency and fiat currency.
Please note that some payment methods may be restricted in your country.
How to use Quick Buy to purchase cryptocurrency?
Please follow the steps below:
  1. Select your fiat currency and target cryptocurrency.
  2. Enter the purchase amount and select a payment method.
  3. Confirm order details and complete payment.
Cryptocurrency usually arrives within 1 minute, but it may take up to 24 hours.
How long does it usually take for Quick Buy to arrive?
Under normal circumstances:
  • Arrives within 1–5 minutes after payment is completed;
  • Delays of 5–15 minutes may occur during peak hours;
If not received within 30 minutes, please contact customer service.
Why can't I use Quick Buy?
Possible reasons include:
  • KYC identity verification not completed
  • Quick buy is not supported in your region.
  • No payment method linked
  • Account Risk Control Restrictions
  • Order amount exceeds the limit
Is it safe to use the quick buy feature?
Platform provides:
  • Merchant Deposit Mechanism
  • Platform Managed Assets
  • Risk Control System Monitoring
  • Customer Service Appeal Channel
Ensure transaction security.
